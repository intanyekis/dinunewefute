Four divers found alive off North Carolina coast, Coast Guard reports WSOC TV

Publish date: 2024-09-07

NORTH CAROLINA — Monday morning, the four divers reported missing off the North Carolina coast were rescued and reunited with their families, the U.S. Coast Guard says.

ExpandAutoplayImage 1 of 4

Families reuniting with rescued divers, Source: USCG Mid-Atlantic

Investigators say the divers were reported missing Sunday around noon, about 60 miles east of Myrtle Beach.

According to the Coast Guard, the four men were diving from the pleasure craft Big Bill’s.

The Coast Guard staged its search 50 miles south of Cape Fear.
