The Bad Blood between Camille Meyer and Ex Husband Kelsey Grammer

Publish date: 2024-09-08

It has been a while since Camille Meyer has entered another relationship. She is now happily married to David C. Meyer.

But 'happy' might not be the right word to describe her previous marriage, or at least its end.

Her more-than-a-decade-long marriage to ex husband Kelsey Grammer ended in 2011, but that did not stop the bad blood between the two.

The former couple has made less-than-friendly remarks about each other even years after their marriage ended.

A Bitter Ending to Camille Meyer's Marriage with First Husband

Meyer married Kesley in 1997 after meeting each other a year prior. Their Malibu wedding started a relationship that lasted for over 13 years.

During their time together, they had two kids via surrogacy. Their first kid, daughter Mason Olivia Grammer, was born on October 24, 2001.

Jude Gordon Grammar, their son, followed his sister into the world on August 28, 2004.

Camille Meyer with kids from her marriage with ex husband Kelsey Grammer. (Photo: Camille Meyer/Instagram)

But things started going downhill soon after, or at least that's what Kelsey has recounted.

During an interview with Graham Bensinger in 2019, Kelsey revealed how things had started getting bad with his then-wife Meyer.

He told Bensinger that Meyer chose a bad day to bring up divorce. Meyer apparently chose the day of Kelsey's mother's funeral to say she wanted a divorce.

However, that wasn't the first time they had that conversation. "We'd had the same conversation for eight years, and it was tiresome," said Kelsey.

"I realized at that moment that I was done with her, that I was not going to end up in this relationship for much longer. And somewhere under a year [later], I was done."

This led straight to their divorce, which she initiated.

Meyer filed for divorce from her ex husband in June 2010. She demanded primary physical custody of their two children, child support, and alimony in the filings.

The couple did not have a prenup. So, she could have walked away with over $50 million, as she rejected the previous offer of a $30 million settlement.

Their divorce was finalized in February 2011, but their dispute over money and custody was only officially concluded in December 2012.

Camille Meyer and Kelsey Grammer Had Bad Blood

A few months before Meyer filed for divorce from Kelsey, she had become a part of the inaugural season of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. She became a big name overnight.

But with her growing name also came troubles for her marriage. During the same time, Kelsey had gone away to work on Broadway for a year.

There, he met another woman, a British flight attendant, Kayte Walsh.

And Kelsey apparently told her he wanted the marriage to be done over a phone call.

But she did not hold back when talking about her ex husband in 2014. During an interview with Andy Cohen, he said some unsavory things about him and his behavior towards her.

When Cohen asked if he spoke to her at the time, Meyer responded in the negative.

She also commented on how "terrible" co-parenting with him was, and said, "He has to learn to love his kids more than he hates me."

They hadn't even had a conversation without a mediator since their divorce.

It was now Kelsey's turn to respond. And he did, albeit after five years.

In the same interview with Bensinger in 2019, he also commented on what he thought about his ex wife's actions regarding him.

"Honestly, I don't really talk about her very much, because so much of her life is spent talking about me," quipped Kelsey. "I just think it's sort of pathetic."

Then came the above comments about Meyer being insensitive towards him and his mother.

The RHOBH star, however, strongly denied that she had done anything of that sort.

In a statement she made on Twitter on November 14, 2019, she revealed that Kelsey's recounting of the events were not true in the slightest.

She detailed what had happened during the days of his mom's funeral and accused him of "rewriting history" at the end of the statement.

A year prior to this, she had also opened up on how he used to act like she did not exist and performed inappropriate behavior in front of their kids.

Camille Meyer's Relationship with Current Husband

Five years after her nuptials with Kelsey had officially ended, Meyer met her current husband, David C. Meyer. They first encountered each other in 2016 in Malibu.

They got engaged in 2017 and got married a year later, in 2018, in a beautiful ceremony at a private beach club in Hawaii.

They do not have kids together yet.
