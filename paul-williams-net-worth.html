Paul Hamilton Williams, Jr has an estimated net worth of $14 million. He is also known by the following nicknames; Paul Hamilton Williams, Jr. Paul H. Williams. He is an American musician, songwriter, composer, and actor. He was born on September 19, 1940. He has made most of his wealth form songwriting. He is married to a writer; Mariana Williams and they have two children.

He is a member of the Songwriters Hall of Fame. Paul has written many songs most of which were popular hits of the 1970s. They include; "Rainy Days and Mondays", "You and Me Against the World", "Evergreen" and "We've Only Just Begun". These popular hits that Paul sang were recorded by renowned artists like Helen Reddy, The Carpenters, and Three Dog Night among others. He also made contributions to the Cinderella liberty song You're So Nice to Be Around. This song was nominated for an Oscar Award. His music has also been used in various films like Bugsy Malone and writing and singing on the Phantom of the Paradise.
