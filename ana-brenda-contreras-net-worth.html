Ana Brenda Contreras Wiki Biography

Born Ana Brenda Contreras Pérez on the 24th December 1986 in McAllen, Texas USA, Ana is an actress and singer, best known to the world for her roles in telenovelas, including “Wild at Heart” (2013), and “Unforgivable” (2015), among other appearances, which have confirmed her as one of the most popular latino performers.

Have you ever wondered how rich Ana Brenda Contreras is, as of mid- 2017? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Contreras’ net worth is as high as $10 million, an amount earned through her successful career as an actress, which started in 2003. In addition to acting, Ana has released an album with the group T de Tila, which also improved her wealth.

Ana Brenda Contreras Net Worth $10 Million

When Ana was 15 years old, she moved to Mexico City, where she became a participant in the reality TV show “Pop Stars”. Her singing talents brought her early fame as she reached the finals of the show., and following the show, she was teamed up with several other contestants to form the group T d Etila, with which she released a self-titled album, the sales of which only increased her net worth.

In 2003 she began studying acting by enrolling at CEA Televisa, and just two years later, she was cast in her first telenovela “Barrera de amor”. The following year, she played Sonia in soap opera “Duel of Passions”, produced by Juan Osorio. Taking steps forward, Ana starred in the film “Divina Confusion” in 2008, and the same year was also selected for the role of Violeta Madrigal in the telenovela “Juro que te amo” (2008-2009), increasing her net worth to a large degree.

Two years later, she was cast in the role of Aurora Alcazar in “Teresa” (2010-2011), appearing next to Sebastián Rulli, Aarón Díaz and Angelique Boyer, and then starred in “The One Who Couldn’t Love” (2011-2012). Little by little, Ana started acquiring lead roles in rather successful telenovelas, such in “Wild at Heart” (2013), in which she played Maricruz – which is a remake of the even more successful telenovela “Marimar” – next to Daniel Arenas. Then in 2015, a new lead role came her way – this time she played Verónica in “Unforgivable” (2015), with Iván Sánchez, Grettell Valdez and Gaby Mellado. Most recently she was chosen for the role of Goyita Vera in the TV biography series about Mexican wrestler Blue Demon, which is also adding to her net worth.

Regarding her personal life, Ana has been in a romantic relationship with her “Unforgivable” co-star Iván Sánchez since 2016. Previously, she was married to celebrity matador Alejandro Amaya from 2013 until 2014 – no reason was evident for the rather swift break-up.
