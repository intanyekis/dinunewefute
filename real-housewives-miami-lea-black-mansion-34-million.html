'Real Housewives Of Miami' Star Lea Black Lists Mansion For $34 Million

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Lea Black, the former reality TV star from The Real Housewives of Miami, has just resurfaced. This time, it's not for a show. It's more of a 'showcase.' She has just listed her enormous mansion for sale for a whopping $34 million, and the more that's discovered about this property, the more intriguing it becomes. TMZ reports this huge price tag is a small price to pay for the fancy surroundings and the very famous neighbors that surround the star's former home. Those with the budget will certainly not want to miss out on the chance to live in the lap of luxury known as Lea Black's life.

There are mansions, and there are mega-mansions, and Lea Black definitely has the latter. Not much has been heard of the reality TV star in recent months, and now it has become clear to fans that it's because she had no real reason to ever leave her luxurious home. Nestled in the very highly esteemed area of Star Island, Miami, Lea's mansion is every bit as elaborate as the neighborhood's name suggests.

Lea and her husband, defense attorney Roy Black, have been quietly living their best lives in one of the most truly admired neighborhoods that most people could only ever dream of. Their home can now be yours, if $34 million is within budget.

As reported by TMZ, this 7 bedroom, 7 bathroom mansion is centralized in an area that is "home to Alex Rodriguez, Diddy, Shaquille O'Neal, Naomi Campbell, Gloria and Emilio Estefan," and that's just to name a few. It's not every day that a walk through the neighborhood packs the same star power as a red carpet event - unless you live here.

The proud new owner of this home will be able to enjoy a luxurious lifestyle that boasts more amenities than can be listed. In fact, the Blacks spent a fortune renovating and re-renovating the home during their 11 year ownership of the property, and the modern state of the sprawling estate are certainly reflective of that.

The home is a 10,636-square-foot extravagant mansion with oversized rooms, expensive detailing, state-of-the-art appliances, and an abundance of luxuries. It comes equipped with an oversized pool which can be seen from any one of the multiple verandas that overlook the enormous property.

Also visible from the terraces are the 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom guest house which is nicer than most mansions inhabited by other celebrities.

The size of the property is an enormous 46,429 square feet, which as TMZ reports, "includes 190 feet of waterfront and a full dock tucked away from visitors on boat tours who want a peek into celebrity life."
