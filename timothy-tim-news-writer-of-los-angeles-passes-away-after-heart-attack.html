Timothy Tim, News writer of Los Angeles passes away after heart attack

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Los Angeles, CA – The Los Angeles community is reeling from the tragic loss of Tim Greenwood, a well-known news writer, reporter, and airborne reporter. Tim, affectionately known to many as Timothy Greenwood, was a beloved resident of Los Angeles, California, and his passing was announced yesterday afternoon. He succumbed to a heart attack, leaving his friends, colleagues, family, and community in deep sorrow.

According to reports, Tim Greenwood’s sudden and unexpected passing occurred as he was diligently working with a colleague, loading his truck with video equipment. The shocking collapse took him from this world, leaving a void that will be difficult to fill. The thoughts and prayers of many are with his friends, coworkers, and loved ones as they navigate this profoundly challenging time.

Tim Greenwood was born in Denver, Colorado, and later made his home in Los Angeles, California. He was a graduate of the University of Northern Colorado in Greeley, CO. Those who knew him remember Tim as a wonderfully kind, old-fashioned, and wholesome gentleman, known for his repertoire of dad jokes and puns. Throughout his career, Tim achieved numerous honors, worked in various production roles, and graced the airwaves as a performer for several networks, including HSN, news, and radio. He possessed a unique ability to connect with people, leaving a lasting impact on those fortunate enough to cross his path.

As Los Angeles mourns the loss of Tim Greenwood, they remember not only his professional contributions but also his warm and engaging personality. His legacy as a respected news reporter and a genuine, kind-hearted individual will continue to live on in the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing him.
