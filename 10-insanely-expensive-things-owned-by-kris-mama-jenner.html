Kris Jenner is a strong businesswoman, television personality, author, and Executive Producer. After she starred on the reality TV show Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Jenner shot to fame with her daughters, who have become the most influential stars in the world. Being their manager, Jenner has earned a 10% cut from every venture, endorsement, and partnership her daughters have done over the years. The mom of six is one of the most powerful women in Hollywood who has used her talent and thoughtful decision-making to make her daughters successful. Being a celebrity herself, Jenner loves to spend money buying expensive luxuries for herself and her family.

Kris Jenner loves traveling and living in pure comfort, besides stunning cars, luxury homes, expensive gifts, and jewelry. Here are the star's most expensive purchases.

Hidden Hills Mansion

In November 2021, Kris Jenner moved into her new Hidden Hills home located in a gated community in Los Angeles. The home is situated on the same lot as Khloe Kardashian’s home. They collectively paid $37 million for the property, and Jenner purchased hers for $20 million. The home construction was documented, and the Momager’s place has dark grey brickwork with a grassy backyard, pool, and shrubbery, as noted by Daily Mail.

Robert Kardashian’s Calabasas Home

The second property on the list is a Calabasas Home that Kris Jenner paid for to buy Robert Kardashian a home. The four-bedroom home was purchased for a $2.28 million price tag, and it is located close to the rest of the Kardashian-Jenner household. The home is designed in warm and neutral tones with an oversized master bedroom with a luxurious bathroom and office space.

Vacation Home

The third real estate purchase made by Kris Jenner is an expensive vacation home she bought in November 2020. Located in La Quinta, a city within the Coachella Valley, the seven-bedroom, ten-bathroom home has a contemporary interior and an eye-watering backyard with a pool that offers scenic views of the golf course. With multiple fire pits and seating areas, the home is the perfect spot for the family to meet and stay before attending the Coachella Festival.

Rolls Royce Ghost

Known to cruise from expensive rides, Kris Jenner took exclusivity to the next level when she purchased the first Rolls Royce Ghost in North America, as stated by People. In November 2020, the first Ghost was delivered to Jenner in Palm Springs when Thanksgiving was right around the corner. The 2021 Ghost came with a string of improvements, including an all-aluminum spaceframe, rear-wheel steering, and all-wheel drive. The exclusive car cost Jenner $332,500.

Ferrari 488

Kylie Jenner is big on surprises, and for Kris Jenner’s 63rd birthday, she surprised her mother with a $250,000 sparkling red Ferrari 488 Gran Turismo. Kylie, Kris' Daughter documented the entire experience of giving her mother the gift, and Kris Jenner burst into tears after seeing the car sitting in her garage. She sat in the sports car with her boyfriend Corey Gamble and gave it a test drive.

Range Rover Autobiography

The star is also a proud owner of a Range Rover Autobiography that comes with a powerful V8 engine. Many years ago, Jenner purchased it for $144,500 and decided to give it a makeover in 2015. She took the car to the West Coast Customs to give it a black matte finish, making it look brand new.

Jewelry Collection

Kris Jenner loves jewelry. She is often spotted wearing Chanel jewelry while on the set of the reality show Keeping Up With The Kardashians. Most notably, Jenner is seen wearing Chanel drop earrings from various collections that cost upwards of $12,000. Taking inspiration from her fashion style, she also launched her Kris Jenner Signature Collection with a starting price of $150.

Clothing And Handbags

Kris Jenner’s fashion inspiration is her mother, Mary Jo. Over the years, Kris Jenner’s style has revolutionized from wearing silk dresses to bespoke coats and blazers from brands such as Burberry, Tom Ford, and Alexander McQueen. According to The Sun, a lover of handbags, Jenner was recently spotted carrying a $100,000 Hermès crocodile leather bag with a diamond-encrusted exterior that costs an additional $480,000.

Watch Collection

Watches are an important accessory to complete a perfect look. Kris Jenner loves sporting expensive watches on red carpets and vacations. A fan of Rolex, most of her timepieces are from the same watchmaker. In 2020, Kris Jenner was seen relaxing in her home backyard wearing a Rolex Cosmograph Daytona Rose Gold that costs upwards of a whopping $35,000.

Gifts For Grandchildren

Kris Jenner loves spoiling her grandchildren when celebrating important milestones in their lives. As mentioned by The Things, Jenner purchased a Louis Vuitton music box costing $3,350 for Kim Kardashian and Kanye West’s daughter Chicago’s birthday. For Kylie Jenner’s daughter, Stormi, she gifted her a life-sized playhouse similar to the one Kylie had growing up.

From owning luxury homes in exclusive neighborhoods in Los Angeles to splashing big on diamonds and handbags, Kris Jenner loves to shower herself with gifts ever so often. Being a supportive mother, she is showered with presents from her daughters, who appreciate their lavish gifts.
