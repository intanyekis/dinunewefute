Chloe Bennet is an American actor, voice actor, and singer who is best known for playing Skye/Daisy Johnson/Quake in the ABC superhero drama series Marvel’s Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. She has also been cast in many other shows and movies such as Jake and the Never Land Pirates, The Nightlife, Marvel Rising: Heart of Iron, The Birthday Boys, Valley Girl, Tinker Bell and the Legend of the NeverBeast, Nostradamus, Abominable, Marvel Rising: Secret Warriors, 5 Years Apart, Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.: Slingshot, Marvel Rising: Initiation, and Marvel Rising: Chasing Ghosts.
