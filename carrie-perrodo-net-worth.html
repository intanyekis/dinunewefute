French businesswoman, Carrie Perrodo, has an estimated net worth of $4.2 billion as of January 2016, according to Forbes. Born in Singapore, Perrodo is a former model who currently owns the the oil group, Perenco. She is the founder of Carrie's Models, a modeling agency which she later sold.

Carrie Perrodo was married to a French businessman named Hubert Perrodo until the latter's death in 2006. Her husband was the founder and the sole owner Perenco, which was considered as one of the world's largest family-owned oil companies. He was known to be an avid collector of artworks, especially paintings. The couple has three children together named Francois, Nathalie, and Bertrand. Currently, Francois serves as the Chairman of the company, while Nathalie takes care of the family's wine investments.
