Britney Could Be Placed In A Conservatorship Again

The Guardian recently reported that there’s been a renewed surge in online activity surrounding the #FreeBritney movement after the highly publicized altercation at the Chateau Marmont.

The publication suggests numerous fans of Britney see the Chateau Marmont episode as a potential effort to reinstate a conservatorship over her, emphasizing the growing concern among her supporters who are vigilant against any actions that might undermine her autonomy.

“Those same fan accounts were stirring anew, prompting a resurgence of an online FreeBritney movement that she credited with helping to end her conservatorship,” the article said. “They see the Chateau Marmont incident as part of an effort to place her back under that control. Dueling narratives emerged. Some said conservatorship also exposed her to the same forces that may have triggered it in the first place.”

Moreover, reports say Britney’s team is growing increasingly concerned and considering their limited options. One report claims her dad Jamie Spears , who was formerly her conservator, may even get involved.

“Britney seemed completely out of it,” a source who witnessed the Chateau Marmont debacle told In Touch. “If she wasn’t high on something she was definitely off her meds.”

“She has no concept of money,” another source told Page Six. “For over a decade, other people were in charge of her bank accounts, and every purchase had to be reported to the court — even if it was just a pack of gum. But now, she’s on her own.” They added, “It’s tricky. No one wants to be Jamie 2.0.”
