The late singer became very close to Snow’s biological mother at the rehabilitation center. Seeing how she was struggling financially to meet her needs, Lisa Lopes offered to adopt her daughter to lessen her burden. This was what led to the commencement of the adoption process of Snow Lopes in 2001 and her relocation from Honduras to the United States.

Unfortunately for Snow Lopes, she lost her adoptive mother to an accident the following year. However, some reports have it that Lisa Lopes didn’t finalize the adoption process before her death.
