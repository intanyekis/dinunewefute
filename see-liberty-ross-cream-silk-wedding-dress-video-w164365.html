See Liberty Ross' Cream Silk Wedding Dress: Video

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Here comes the bride, all dressed in white! Liberty Ross chose a stunning silk dress to marry Jimmy Iovine in a star-studded Valentine’s Day ceremony on Sunday, February 14, in Beverly Hills.

The British model, 37, donned a cream-colored gown with a draped halter neckline and train, teamed with diamond earrings, for her beachside wedding to the famed music producer, 62. Naturally, she skipped the shoes, making it easier for her to stroll through the sand and into the ocean, where she tossed a large garland of white flowers.

Ross styled her raven tresses into loose curls, and modeled minimal makeup — just sun-kissed cheeks and a natural, rose-hued lip.

One blonde pal close to Ross — who became a media focus when Us Weekly exclusively revealed that her ex-husband, Rupert Sanders, was having an affair with his Snow White and the Huntsman leading lady, Kristen Stewart, in 2012 — wore a slinky, periwinkle blue bridesmaid dress (also sans shoes).

Ross’ groom looked handsome in a slate suit with a white button-up shirt, paired with black sneakers.

The guests, however, received a festive memo! Asked to wear red for the holiday, Oprah Winfrey, Gayle King and Helen Williams (Pharrell Williams‘ wife) all slipped into scarlet dresses for the special occasion.
