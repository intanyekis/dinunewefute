Phil Hartman Left His Fortune To The Person Who Took His Life

Often heralded as one of the funniest cast members in Saturday Night Live history, new generations of comedy fans get to discover Phil Hartman. Once a new fan discovers how hilarious Hartman was, they are invariably left with one question. How did Phil Hartman die? As it turns out, Hartman left his fortune to the same person who took his life.

On May 28, 1998, the world became a lot less funny when Phil Hartman tragically passed away because his wife Brynn Hartman took his life. For fans of the comedy genius, it was an extremely hard pill to swallow that the actor was gone.

Within days of Hartman's tragic passing, his fans were hit with another blow when news about his estate became public. According to a Los Angeles Times report from June 3rd, 1998, Phil Hartman left his entire estate to his wife who took his life.

"'NewsRadio' star Phil Hartman left everything to his wife, who k*lled him and then herself last week at the couple’s Encino estate, according to copies of the couple’s wills filed Tuesday for probate in Los Angeles Superior Court."
