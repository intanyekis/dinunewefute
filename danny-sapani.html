Danny Sapani is a talented British actor who is known for his roles in Misfits, Doctor Who, Penny Dreadful, The Bastard Executioner, The Crown, Ultimate Force, The Bill, MotherFatherSon, Star Wars: The Last Jedi, Black Panther, and Tarakasura. He is a trained Shakespearean actor and played the roles of Macbeth in an award-winning production directed by Max Stafford Clark. Danny took acting lessons from the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama in London, England.
